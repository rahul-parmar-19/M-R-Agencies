Our Legacy

Chennai's Premier Conglomerate

M R Agencies was founded in 2001 by visionary entrepreneur Rajendra Kumar Bagrecha with the core belief of delivering excellence, value, and long-term trust to all stakeholders.

Over two decades, we have diversified from consumer product distribution to luxury real estate developments, high-value precious metals trade, and technology-driven healthcare investments.

Premium Standards

Uncompromising commitment to luxury, ethics, and corporate transparency.

Trusted Networks

Long-term strategic relationships with India's largest builders and trade partners.

Milestones & Growth

2001
Incorporated as a premium consumer distribution firm in Chennai, leading marketing for watch brands and home electronics.
2008
Expanded footprints to multi-state operations (Karnataka, Gujarat, Rajasthan) and ventured into gold & silver imports/exports.
2018
Partnered with SPR City for Market of India and Sky Towers projects, transforming Chennai's retail and luxury living skyline.
Present
Scaling smart townships, real estate developments, and investing in advanced healthcare technology systems.
